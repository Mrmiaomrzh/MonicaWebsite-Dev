# 🛡️ Monica Privacy Policy

::: note Policy Preamble
This Privacy Policy clarifies the standards adhered to by `Monica` when processing, storing, and transferring your personal data. As an independent software developed around the core philosophy of **"Local-First, Zero-Knowledge Protection"**, we are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy security.
:::

## 1. Google Drive Authorization <Badge text="On-Demand Trigger" type="tip" />
Monica will only access Google Drive upon explicit user consent and authorization. Without authorization, the App will never actively connect to or read your Google Drive data.

---

## 2. Scope of Access <Badge text="Minimized Permissions" type="info" />
Monica only operates within folders explicitly selected by the user or on files created by Monica itself for backup/sync purposes. It will never access any other file contents unrelated to your actual use.

---

## 3. Credentials & Account Security <Badge text="Zero Storage" type="warning" />
Monica never stores your Google account password and will never request login credentials for third-party platforms. Authorization tokens are strictly used to fulfill sync workflows initiated by you and can be revoked at any time.

---

## 4. Data Usage & Sharing <Badge text="Highest Defense Line" type="danger" />

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Commercial Ethics & Data Compliance Commitment**
> Monica will never sell your data, and absolutely never shares your personal data with third parties. Your data is exclusively used to power features actively triggered by you (e.g., backup, restore, and synchronization).

---

## 5. User Data Control
You retain full control over your data. You can stop using associated features, delete cloud backup files, or revoke Monica's access permissions at any time within your Google Account Access Management (<kbd>Security</kbd> ➔ <kbd>Third-party apps with account access</kbd>).

---

## 6. Contact Information
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to the project maintainer via the <a href="https://github.com/JoyinJoester/Monica" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Monica GitHub Project Page</a>.
